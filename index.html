Setup Firebase (Free)
Firebase is Google's free database — free forever, no credit card needed. Follow these steps:
Step 1: Go to console.firebase.google.com
Step 2: Click "Create a project" → name it "a2dv-fund" → Continue
Step 3: Disable Google Analytics (not needed) → "Create project"
Step 4: Click "Firestore Database" in left sidebar
Step 5: Click "Create database" → choose "Start in test mode" → pick any location → Enable
Step 6: Go to Project Settings (gear icon) → scroll down → click "</> Web" → register app → copy the firebaseConfig object

Takes about 3 minutes. It's completely free forever.

Paste Firebase Config
Paste your firebaseConfig values below. They look like this:
apiKey: "AIzaSy..."
authDomain: "a2dv-fund.firebaseapp.com"
projectId: "a2dv-fund"
...
